## Participant Requirements
- Age: 18-70
- Own a smartwatch (Apple/Fitbit/Garmin/Samsung/...)
- Willing to listen to a 30 minute playlist for at least 10 times over 3 months

## Study Flow

### Week 1: Onboarding
1. Participant registers via Google Form
2. We send informed consent + instructions
3. They sign consent → assigned P-[XXX] code
4. First biometric baseline measurement (no music)

### Weeks 2-22: Data Collection (bi-weekly)
Each session:
1. Participant exports smartwatch data (last 2 weeks)
2. Participant listens to assigned playlist (30 min)
3. Participant fills context form (mood before/after)
4. Participant uploads data

### Playlist Assignment
- Sessions 1-7: Baseline playlist (neutral)
- Sessions 8-14: Calm playlist (stress reduction)
- Sessions 15-22: Upbeat playlist (energy boost)

## Data Collected
- Smartwatch: HR, HRV, sleep, stress, steps
- Spotify: Listening history (timestamps)
- Self-report: Mood, energy, stress (1-10 scales)
